Introduction to Manga

Manga is an umbrella term for a wide variety of comic books and graphic novels originally produced and published in Japan. Unlike American comic books, which are usually printed in full color, Japanese manga are almost always published in black and white. If you'd lke to know more about what Manga is and where it comes from, click the read more link below.
